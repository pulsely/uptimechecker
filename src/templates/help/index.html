Welcome to Uptime Checker

The aim of Uptime Checker is to provide a reliable and efficient solution for monitoring website uptimes and SSL expiration dates. With our application, you can easily track the availability of your websites and receive timely notifications about any potential issues.

Website uptime is crucial for online businesses, as even a minor downtime can result in missed opportunities and customer dissatisfaction. The Pulsely Uptime Checker leverages the power of Django, a high-level Python web framework, to create a robust monitoring system that regularly sends HTTP requests to the specified websites. By periodically checking the status of these websites, our application ensures that you stay informed about any downtime occurrences.

SSL/TLS expiration checks

Uptime Checker also offers SSL certificate expiration checks. SSL certificates play a vital role in establishing secure connections between web servers and clients. It is essential to keep track of their expiration dates to maintain the security of your website.

E-mail notifications will be sent to give you warnings whenever a website goes down or an SSL certificate is nearing its expiration date. By promptly addressing these issues, you can ensure that your websites are always up and running smoothly, providing a seamless experience for your users.

The Pulsely Uptime Checker is an Open Source project

The open-source nature of Pulsely Uptime Checker means that you can freely access, modify, and contribute to its development. We believe in the power of collaboration and community-driven innovation, and we invite developers from all backgrounds to join us in improving this Django-based monitoring solution. By working together, we can make it even more feature-rich, scalable, and adaptable to various environments.
